Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Green Empire Builders's services on Long Island.

Green Empire Builders serves Nassau and Suffolk Counties including Massapequa, Merrick, Bellmore, Wantagh, Seaford, Freeport, Baldwin, Oceanside, Rockville Centre, Levittown, Amityville, Lindenhurst, Babylon, and more. Not sure if we cover your area? Call or text us at (516) 595-0684.

Every project is different, which is why we start with a free on-site consultation. We assess the scope, your goals, and your property before providing a detailed written quote. Factors that affect cost include square footage, material selections, structural changes, and permit requirements. We never start work without your full pricing approval.

In most cases, a well-planned renovation or addition is more cost-effective than buying a larger home on Long Island. You keep your location, your neighborhood, and your equity while gaining the space and features you want. We can help you evaluate whether an addition, a second story, or a full renovation makes the most sense for your situation.

Yes. Permit coordination is included in every project. We manage the full permitting process with your local building department so you do not have to. Work begins only after all required approvals are in place.

Yes. Second-story additions are one of our specialties. We assess your existing foundation and structure, coordinate engineering if needed, and build the addition to match the character of your home. Timelines depend on scope. A bathroom remodel typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. A kitchen renovation runs 4 to 8 weeks. A home addition or second story can take 3 to 6 months. We provide a realistic timeline before work begins and keep you updated throughout.
